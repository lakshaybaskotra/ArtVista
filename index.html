Claude Monet

A Japanese contemporary artist known for her polka-dot installations, sculptures, and paintings. Her work explores themes of infinity, obsession, and the self, and is often described as playful and mesmerizing.

Frederic Edwin Church

He was a renowned American landscape painter of the 19th century. He is considered a master of Luminism, a style known for its meticulous detail, focus on light and atmosphere.
